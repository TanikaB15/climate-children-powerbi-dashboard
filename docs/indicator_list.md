# Indicator List: How Climate Impacts Children

| Layer | Indicator | Plain-language meaning | Suggested data column name | Example unit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Exposure Layer | Floods | Measures how much a country or area is exposed to flooding or flood events. | `flood_exposure` | Index score, number of flood events, or % population exposed |
| Exposure Layer | Temperature anomalies | Shows how much temperature differs from the normal or historical average. | `temperature_anomaly` | Degrees Celsius above/below baseline |
| Exposure Layer | Rainfall changes | Shows whether rainfall is increasing, decreasing, or becoming unusual compared with normal patterns. | `rainfall_change` | % change from baseline or millimetres |
| Vulnerability Layer | Food insecurity | Measures how many people have unreliable or insufficient access to food. | `food_insecurity` | % of population |
| Vulnerability Layer | Undernourishment | Measures the share of people whose food intake is not enough to meet basic dietary energy needs. | `undernourishment` | % of population |
| Vulnerability Layer | Unsafe water exposure | Measures exposure to unsafe drinking water, poor sanitation, or unsafe water conditions. | `unsafe_water_exposure` | % of population |
| Child Outcome Layer | Under-5 mortality | Measures deaths of children before their fifth birthday. | `under5_mortality` | Deaths per 1,000 live births |
| Child Outcome Layer | Infant mortality | Measures deaths of children before their first birthday. | `infant_mortality` | Deaths per 1,000 live births |
| Child Outcome Layer | Neonatal mortality | Measures deaths of babies during the first 28 days of life. | `neonatal_mortality` | Deaths per 1,000 live births |
| Child Outcome Layer | Child stunting | Measures the share of children who are too short for their age due to long-term undernutrition. | `child_stunting` | % of children under 5 |
| Child Outcome Layer | Child labour | Measures the share or number of children involved in work that may harm their health, education, or development. | `child_labour` | % of children or number of children |
